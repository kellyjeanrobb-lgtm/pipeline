Pipeline Runway — Formula Comparison

The pipeline length number looks different than before because the formula changed. The table and chart below show both measures side by side across the full dataset.

Old Formula Result vs. New 6-Mo Rolling View (Aug 2023 – Feb 2026)

Old formula swings from −270 to +371 across two consecutive months with no real change in pipeline composition. New 6-mo rolling stays within a narrow band throughout.

Old Formula  ·  Retired April 2026
3-Mo Avg Pipeline Accounts  ÷  (3-Mo Avg Elections − 3-Mo Avg New Applicants)

The old formula divided 3-month average pipeline accounts by the gap between average elections and average new applicants. When those two inputs converged, the denominator approached zero and the result became mathematically unstable.
New Formula  ·  Standard from April 2026
Pipeline Accounts  ÷  Monthly Elections

More straightforward. Confirmed against 6- and 12-month rolling views so one unusual month doesn't distort the picture. The pipeline hasn't changed — the lens is more usable and shows the growth post-COVID.
